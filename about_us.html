About Us

We are students of the Software Engineering Section (M2) from the Department of Software Engineering at IUB (Islamia University Bahawalpur). Our session is from 2023-2027.

This website was created by our classmate, Shahzad Nawaz. We built this website to ensure the safety of our classmates' data. In case any of us leave the university or encounter any problems, we want to prevent unauthorized access and misuse of personal information.

With this website, we aim to provide the following advantages:

  • Accessing contact numbers: If any of our classmates' contact information is not available to someone and they need to get in touch, they can use this website to obtain the contact details.
  • Locating classmates in specific cities: If any of our classmates plan to visit another city and require assistance, they can check this website to find out which classmates reside in that specific city.

We also have a gallery section that includes photos of each classmate. This allows us to reminisce and remember our friends.

We appreciate your understanding and support.

Thank you!

Note: This website is for internal use only and should not be accessed or shared by unauthorized individuals. We prioritize the privacy and security of our classmates' data.
